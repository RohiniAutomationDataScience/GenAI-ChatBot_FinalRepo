GDRFA Dubai Working Hours

The official working hours for the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA) Dubai are:

8:00 AM to 8:00 PM (local time), Sunday to Thursday

Additionally:

The AMER Service Call Centre is available 24/7 throughout the year for inquiries and support.
Amer online chat is available from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Sunday to Thursday, EST).

For further details or confirmation, you may visit the official GDRFA Dubai website or contact their call centre directly:

International: +971 4 313 9999

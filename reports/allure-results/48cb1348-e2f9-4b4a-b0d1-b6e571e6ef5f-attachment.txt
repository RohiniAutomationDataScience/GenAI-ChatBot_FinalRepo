Working Hours of GDRFA Dubai

The official working hours for the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA) Dubai are:

Sunday to Thursday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM

Additionally:

AMER Call Centre (Customer Service): Open 24/7 throughout the year
Amer Online Chat: Available from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (EST), Sunday to Thursday

For more details or updates, you may visit the official GDRFA Dubai website or contact their customer service directly 1.

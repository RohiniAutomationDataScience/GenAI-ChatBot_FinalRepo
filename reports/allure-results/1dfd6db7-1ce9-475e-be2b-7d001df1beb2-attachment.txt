GDRFA Dubai Working Hours

The official working hours for the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA) Dubai are:

8:00 AM to 8:00 PM (local time)

Additionally, the AMER Service Call Centre is available 24/7 throughout the year for inquiries and support. The Amer online chat service operates from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Sunday to Thursday, EST).
